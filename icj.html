International Court of Justice

Ukraine v. Russian Federation is a case brought before the International Court of Justice (ICJ). It was submitted by Ukraine on 26 February 2022 against Russia following the latter's invasion of Ukraine in 2022. Ukraine based its application on the ICJ's jurisdiction to resolve disputes involving the convention, demanding ICJ rule that Russia halts military operations. It is now up to our judges in KARMUN'23 to decide.

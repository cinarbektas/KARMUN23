Disarmamet and International Security

DISEC is the first assembly of United Nations. DISEC is the abbreviation of disarmermant and international security. The Disarmament and International Security Committee focuses maintaining peace and establishing security through tackling down the threats against international security and prosperity such as nuclear, biological and chemical weapons. In this years KARMUN, the delegates are going to be discussing heavily upon dismantling the weapons of mass destruction and establishing stricter regulations against the increasing demand for nuclear energy.

Welcome Letter

Highly esteemed participants on the behalf of the executive team, We welcome you to the second edition of KARMUN. This conference is the result of lots of sleepless nights and the resilience of our executives. When we started working for this conference, the world was a different place. During the time that we spent cultivating this conference from the very scratch, we witnessed a society which was being shaped by various events both good and bad. As the executives we can say that we had to adapt to the ever changing circumstances, in order to put an end to 4 years of waiting. Our dream is to make KARMUN a conference that would reflect the breaking points that constructed the world that we are living in. In this years Karmun, we crafted 3 Crisis Committees, which will push our delegates' problem solving skills to its limit and help them to adapt to every situation that they encounter. Our academic team worked meticulously in order to craft 3 special committees which will broden our delegates horizon and help them towards being one step closer to their academic goals. As KARMUN Team, our sole aim is to cultivate a conference in which our delegates creative ideas will find voice. As executives we hope that during 3 days you are going to be spending, debating, writing and changing the world through your pen and ideas, you will find a piece of yourself in this conference and connect with the like minded individuals. Because we reckon that the change comes from unity when we stand together as one we will have the power towards making our world a better place for everyone regardless of their race, religion or backgrounds.

Zehra Naz Ukşaş   Selim Erdem Yıldırım
